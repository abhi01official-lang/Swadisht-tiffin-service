Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Swadisht Tiffins. These Terms & Conditions govern meal orders, deliveries, cancellations, refunds, wallet usage, and service policies. Please read them carefully to ensure a smooth experience.

1. Ordering & Cut-Off Timings

The cut-off time to place, cancel, or modify orders is:

  • Lunch Orders: Previous day by 8:00 pm or Same Day by 11:00 am
  • Dinner Orders: Same day by 5:00 pm or Before 8:00 pm

Orders cannot be placed, cancelled, or modified once the respective cut-off time has passed.

2. Delivery Timings

  • Lunch Delivery: 12:00 am to 3:30 pm
  • Dinner Delivery: 7:30 pm to 11:30 pm

Delivery timings may vary slightly due to traffic conditions, route schedules, or delivery partner constraints.

3. Area Serviceability Confirmation

  • Customers are requested to confirm delivery availability in their area before placing an order.
  • If an order is placed for a non-serviceable area, it may be cancelled and refunded as per the applicable refund mode.

4. Premises Delivery Clause

During lunch deliveries, our delivery partners may leave tiffins at a common delivery location such as:

  • Reception
  • Security gate
  • Canteen
  • Common area / near service lift

Customers may confirm whether delivery will be direct or at a common point before ordering.

5. Pricing, Delivery Charges & Taxes

  • Delivery Charges: are included per order (not applies separately for lunch and dinner)
  • GST: we are not under gst yet but - (5% applicable on the total order value, excluding delivery charges)

6. Packaging & Food Temperature

  • Meals are packed in disposable, microwave-safe containers.
  • Meals are cooled at room temperature before packing to ensure food safety.
  • Customers may receive food at room temperature; remove the lid and re-heat before consumption for best taste.

7. Cancellation & Refund Terms

  • Customers can cancel meals before cut-off time via whatsapp/call.
  • Cancelled meals are not carried forward to future dates.
  • Refunds for cancelled meals go instantly to the bank account.
  • Permanent discontinuation refund requests are processed to bank accounts after verification.

8. bank account refund Terms

9. No-Delivery Situations

  • No lunch delivery on Sundays, strikes, heavy rains, natural disasters, riots, or circumstances beyond control.
  • Customers will be informed in advance for planned disruptions.
  • Food Donation Clause: Prepared meals in unavoidable no-delivery cases may be donated to charity. In such cases, the order amount is non-refundable.

10. Delivery Responsibility Disclaimer

Deliveries are handled by Swadisht atiffins. Swadisht Tiffins coordinates delivery but is liable for disputes or delays.

11. Order Details & Booking History

  • Customers should check Booking History on the whatsapp/call for verification of orders.
  • Orders are processed strictly as per visible booking history.

12. Company Rights

  • Swadisht Tiffins may cancel/modify meals due to operational constraints.
  • Service may be discontinued to any customer at the company’s discretion.
  • Where possible, customers will be informed in advance.
