About Swadisht Tiffin – Meerut

Swadisht Tiffin is a trusted home-style tiffin service in Meerut, started with one simple goal – to provide fresh, hygienic, and tasty homemade food at an affordable price. We understand the daily struggle of students, working professionals, bachelors, and senior citizens when it comes to healthy meals.

Our food is prepared fresh every day in a clean kitchen using quality ingredients, traditional spices, and balanced recipes – so every bite feels like ghar ka khana, not hotel food.

What Makes Swadisht Tiffin Special?

  • 100% Home-Cooked Meals
  • Freshly Prepared Every Day
  • Affordable Price – Only ₹80 per Meal
  • No Artificial Colors or Preservatives
  • Clean & Hygienic Kitchen
  • Timely Tiffin Delivery Across Meerut
